Arsenal 5 - 1 Manchester City

Arsenal delivered a stunning performance in Matchweek 24 of the 2024-25 Premier League, beating reigning champions Manchester City 5–1 at home — one of their biggest ever wins over City and their biggest against the title holders in Premier League history.

Key moments & goals

2′ – Martin Ødegaard opened the scoring early for Arsenal.

55′ – Erling Haaland equalised for Man City with a header.

56′ – Thomas Partey regained the lead.

62′ – Myles Lewis-Skelly struck with his first senior goal.

76′ – Kai Havertz added a fourth.

90+3′ – Ethan Nwaneri sealed the rout with a stoppage-time finish.

Inter Milan 4 - 3 Barcelona

Inter produced an unforgettable night in the 2024–25 Champions League semi-final second leg, coming from behind to beat Barcelona 4–3 after extra time at the San Siro in a dramatic encounter that saw late goals, stunning saves, and a decisive extra-time strike to seal a 7–6 aggregate victory and send the Italians into the final.

Key moments & goals

21′ — Lautaro Martínez opens the scoring with a clinical finish.

45+1′ — Hakan Çalhanoğlu converts a penalty just before half-time to make it 2-0.

54′ — Eric García pulls one back with a powerful volley.

60′ — Dani Olmo heads home to level it at 2-2 on the night.

87′ — Raphinha slots in what looks like the winning goal to put Barça ahead 3-2.

90+3′ — Francesco Acerbi scores a dramatic stoppage-time equalizer to make it 3-3 — forcing extra time.

99′ — Davide Frattesi curls in the winner to make it 4-3 on the night and send Inter into the final.
